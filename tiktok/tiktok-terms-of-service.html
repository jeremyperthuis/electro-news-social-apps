02

Description of the Application

electro-news-insights is a content analytics tool designed for media and editorial teams. It allows authorized users to monitor the performance of their organization's TikTok content in one place, without leaving their workflow.

The App connects to the TikTok API using OAuth 2.0 authentication. It accesses, for the authenticated TikTok account belonging to our organization, the following data using the scopes below:

video.list – list of published videos video.list.stats – total like count per video video.list.stats – total comment count per video video.list.stats – total share count per video

Only the minimum scopes necessary to deliver the app's core functionality are requested. No other data is accessed, stored, or processed. The App does not access follower lists, private messages, user demographics, or any data from accounts other than our own.

03

Data Collection & Use

The App collects only aggregate, non-personal statistics related to content published on our own TikTok profile. Specifically:

  • Video identifiers and publication dates
  • Video titles and descriptions (for identification purposes)
  • Aggregate engagement metrics: total likes, total comments, total shares

This data is used exclusively for content performance analysis by our editorial team. It is never sold, licensed, shared with, or disclosed to any third party.

We do not collect, process, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) about TikTok users who may have interacted with our content.

04

Data Storage & Retention

Retrieved metrics may be stored in secure databases or spreadsheets accessible only to authorized members of the analytics team. Data is retained for as long as necessary for performance analysis and is deleted upon request or when no longer needed.

Access to stored data is restricted by role-based permissions. No data is exposed publicly or to unauthorized personnel.
