Privacy Policy

electro-news-insights  ·  Effective date: April 23, 2026  ·  v2.0

Plain-English Summary

electro-news-insights lets any TikTok creator or media team connect their TikTok account and visualise the performance of their own videos — likes, comments and shares — in one dashboard. We only access the data you explicitly authorise via TikTok's official OAuth flow. We never access data from other users' accounts and we never sell your data.

01

Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to electro-news-insights (the "App"), a TikTok content analytics platform operated by Electro News (Paris, France). The App is available to any TikTok creator or organisation wishing to monitor the performance of their own TikTok content.

Our official website is https://electro-news.eu. For any privacy-related inquiries, please use the contact details at the bottom of this document.

02

What Data We Access

When you connect your TikTok account to the App, we request the minimum API scopes necessary to provide the service:

video.list — your published videos video.list.stats — likes, comments & shares per video

All data accessed belongs exclusively to your own TikTok account. We never access data from any other TikTok user's account.

We do not access: your follower list, private messages, account demographics, personally identifiable information of other TikTok users, or any content you have not published yourself.

03

Personal Data We Collect

To provide the service, we collect the following information:

  • Your TikTok open ID, used to associate your analytics data with your account
  • Your TikTok OAuth 2.0 access token, stored securely and used solely to retrieve your video statistics
  • Your email address, if provided voluntarily for account notifications or support requests
  • Basic video metadata: video ID, title, publication date, and aggregate engagement counts (likes, comments, shares)

We do not collect passwords, payment information, location data, device identifiers, or behavioral tracking data. We do not store video files.

04

How We Use Your Data

The data collected is used exclusively to:

  • Display your video performance metrics in your personal analytics dashboard
  • Generate engagement trend charts over time for your published content
  • Send optional account notifications if you have opted in

Your data is never used for advertising, profiling, machine learning training, or any purpose other than providing you the analytics service you signed up for.

05

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, license, or share your data with any third party under any circumstances. We do not use third-party advertising networks, behavioral tracking tools, or data brokers. No external vendor or service provider has access to your TikTok data.

06

Data Storage & Retention

Your analytics data is stored in our secure infrastructure, accessible only to authorised personnel. Data is retained for as long as your account is active.

When you disconnect your TikTok account from the App or delete your account, all associated data and access tokens are permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days.

07

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted storage of OAuth access tokens and API credentials
  • All communications with the TikTok API conducted over HTTPS
  • Access to production systems restricted to authorised personnel only
  • Regular review of access permissions and security configurations

In the event of a security incident affecting your data, we will notify you and TikTok as required under the TikTok API Developer Terms of Service and applicable data protection regulations (including GDPR) within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

08

TikTok API & Third-Party Services

The App uses TikTok's official API in compliance with the TikTok API Developer Terms of Service. TikTok's own privacy practices govern the data held on their platform. We encourage you to review TikTok's Privacy Policy. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of TikTok or any other third-party service.

09

Your Rights

As a user of the App, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data and account
  • Withdraw your TikTok authorisation at any time via TikTok's app settings
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (EU residents: CNIL for France)

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the address below. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

10

Cookies & Tracking

The App may use essential session cookies solely to maintain your logged-in state. No advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioral analytics tools are used.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date and version number at the top of this page will be updated accordingly. For material changes, we will notify registered users by email where possible.
