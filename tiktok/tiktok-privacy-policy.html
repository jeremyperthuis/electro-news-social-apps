Privacy Policy

electro-news-insights  ·  Effective date: April 23, 2026  ·  v1.0

Plain-English Summary

This application connects to the TikTok API to retrieve aggregate performance statistics (likes, comments, shares) from our own TikTok profile, enabling our team to monitor and analyse content performance. No personal data from any TikTok user is collected, stored, or shared with third parties.

01

Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to the TikTok Analytics Application electro-news-insights, operated by Electro News (Paris, France). The App connects to the TikTok API to retrieve performance metrics from our own TikTok account, supporting content analysis and editorial decision-making.

Our official website is available at https://electro-news.eu. For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us at the address provided at the bottom of this document.

02

What Data We Access

The App requests access to the following data via the TikTok API, strictly limited to our own TikTok profile. The following TikTok API scopes are used:

video.list (list of published videos) video.list.stats – total likes per video video.list.stats – total comments per video video.list.stats – total shares per video

These scopes are the minimum required to fulfil the app's purpose. The data retrieved is entirely aggregated and non-personal. It reflects statistics about content we have published ourselves, and does not include any information about the individuals who may have liked, commented on, or shared our videos.

We do not access: follower lists, user profiles, private messages, account demographics, personally identifiable information of any TikTok user, or any data from accounts other than our own.

03

No Personal Data Collected

The App does not collect, process, or store any personally identifiable information (PII). This includes but is not limited to:

  • Names, usernames, or display names of TikTok users
  • Email addresses or phone numbers
  • Profile pictures or bios
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Behavioral or browsing data
  • Any data relating to minors

The only account credentials handled by the App are our own OAuth 2.0 access tokens, used solely to authenticate with the TikTok API on behalf of our organization.

04

How We Use the Data

The statistics retrieved from the TikTok API are used by our editorial and content team for:

  • Performance reporting and content analysis
  • Tracking engagement trends over time on our published content
  • Supporting data-driven editorial and content strategy decisions

The data is never used for advertising, profiling, machine learning training, or any purpose beyond content performance analytics.

05

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, license, or share any data retrieved via the TikTok API with any third party, under any circumstances.

Access to the App and its data is strictly limited to authorized members of our content and analytics team. No external vendor, partner, or service provider has access to this data.

We do not use any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking tools within the App.

06

Data Storage & Retention

Retrieved metrics may be temporarily cached or stored in secure systems (such as private databases or spreadsheets) accessible only to authorized team members.

Data is retained only for as long as it is needed for content performance analysis purposes. When data is no longer required, it is securely deleted.

All stored data remains within our organization's secure infrastructure and is never transferred to external servers or cloud services outside of our control.

07

Security Measures

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the data we retrieve, including:

  • Secure, encrypted storage of OAuth access tokens and API credentials
  • Access restricted to authorized personnel only
  • All communications with the TikTok API conducted over HTTPS
  • Regular review of access permissions and credentials

In the event of a security incident or data breach affecting data retrieved via the TikTok API, we will take immediate corrective action, notify TikTok as required under the TikTok API Developer Terms of Service, and inform any affected parties in accordance with applicable data protection regulations (including GDPR where applicable) within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach.

08

TikTok API & Third-Party Services

The App uses TikTok's official API in compliance with the TikTok API Developer Terms of Service. By using this App, you acknowledge that TikTok's own privacy practices govern the data held on TikTok's platform. We encourage you to review TikTok's Privacy Policy for more information.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of TikTok or any other third-party service.

09

Your Rights

Since the App does not collect personal data from any individual user, there is generally no personal data for us to provide, correct, or delete upon request.

If you believe we hold any information related to you and wish to exercise your rights under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR for EU residents), please contact us at the address below. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

10

Cookies & Tracking

The App does not use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, or any other tracking technologies. No user session data or browsing behavior is recorded.
