02

Description of the Application

The App is an internal analytics tool that connects to the TikTok API using OAuth 2.0 authentication. Its sole purpose is to retrieve, for the authenticated TikTok account belonging to our organization, the following data:

List of published videos Total like count per video Total comment count per video Total share count per video

No other data is accessed, stored, or processed. The App does not access follower lists, private messages, user demographics, or any data from accounts other than our own.

03

Data Collection & Use

The App collects only aggregate, non-personal statistics related to content published on our own TikTok profile. Specifically:

  • Video identifiers and publication dates
  • Video titles and descriptions (for identification purposes)
  • Aggregate engagement metrics: total likes, total comments, total shares

This data is used exclusively for internal performance analysis by the analytics team. It is never sold, licensed, shared with, or disclosed to any third party.

We do not collect, process, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) about TikTok users who may have interacted with our content.

Data Storage & Retention

Retrieved metrics may be stored in internal databases or spreadsheets accessible only to authorized members of the analytics team. Data is retained for as long as necessary for performance analysis and is deleted upon request or when no longer needed.

Access to stored data is restricted by role-based permissions. No data is exposed publicly or to unauthorized personnel.
