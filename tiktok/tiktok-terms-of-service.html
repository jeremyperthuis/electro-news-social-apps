Description of the Application

electro-news-insights is a content analytics platform that helps TikTok creators and media teams track the performance of their published videos. Any TikTok user can sign in, connect their account, and immediately see engagement metrics for their content in a clear, unified dashboard.

The App connects to TikTok's official API via OAuth 2.0. The following scopes are requested — and only those strictly necessary to deliver the service:

video.list — list your published videos video.list.stats — likes, comments & shares per video

No other data is accessed, stored, or processed. The App does not access follower lists, private messages, user demographics, or data from any account other than the one you connect.
